Population growth impact on GDP

  • Scatterplot of "Population Growth Vs GDP growth rate" depicts the GDP growth rate on the X-axis versus percentage of population growth along the Y-axis and the population as the size of the circles in the scatter plot. The color scheme is based on the population growth size
