Population growth impact on Forest

  • Line chart of percentage of forest area for different countries. X-axis shows the years versus forest area in the Y-axis
  • Visualization presents that forest area has been depleting as the population is growing. As a result of depletion in forest area, environment is negatively impacted like extinction of wildlife, soil erosion, floods, global warming, loss of biodiversity
  • For the purpose of this project dataset is considered from the year 2000 through 2018
